---
title: Small Feedback Loops Win
description: Speed comes from reducing uncertainty, not rushing the wrong work.
cover_image: /assets/images/feedback-loops.svg
---

Teams move fast when they shorten the distance between idea, implementation, and feedback.

The easiest way to start is to reduce batch size:

- ship fewer changes per pull request
- write checks that run in minutes, not hours
- demo partial work early

Small loops feel slower at first, but they almost always produce better outcomes with less rework.
